Strike Info

The goal of this strike is to demonstrate the power a voice can have
By asking people to inspire their online communities with the reasons they support the fight against climate change, we hope for thousands to pledge to commit to a sustainable future

THREE pledges cover FOOD waste FASHION waste and TIME wasing
THREE of the most pressing threats to the current climate crisis

FOOD

The UN believe that nearly a third of the food we produce is wasted, this equates to roughly 740,584,337 Tons of food lost or wasted GLOBALLY THIS YEAR

FASHION

Every year, global emissions from textile production are equivalent to 1.2 billion tonnes of CO2, a figure that outweighs the carbon footprint of international flights and shipping combined

TIME

If you compare Earth’s history to a calendar year than we (humans) have only existed for around 37 minutes - and we have used 33% of Earths entire natural resources in 0.2 seconds. Let’s not waste anymore time - ACT NOW
